Krull: After many years I am returning to my roots in aeronautics. This is the first steps of a computer simulation of blades rotating through a liquid oil. I used Lattice Boltzmann with four billion cells here and will soon scale up to 64 billion cells. However, my real goal is to use the same computer codes to simulate the worlds ocean dynamics.
